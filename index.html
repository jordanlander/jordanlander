About Me

I'm a forward-thinking, data-driven education administrator with over 15 years of experience designing inclusive STEAM curricula, leading change initiatives, and empowering teams. I thrive on integrating technology and creativity to foster growth and equity.

Leadership Highlights

  • Redesigned a virtual learning program, improving student engagement and saving over $800K in outplacement costs.
  • Led development of digital tools that streamlined compliance and collaboration for K–12 staff.
  • Implemented data-driven strategies that boosted reading and math performance on standardized assessments.
  • Built partnerships with families and local organizations to support inclusive learning environments.

Professional Experience

  • Lead strategic vision & daily operations for special education across multiple districts.
  • Manage IEP development, compliance with IDEA/Pennsylvania regs, and transition planning.
  • Supervise & mentor a team of certified teachers & paraprofessionals, fostering a student-centered culture.
  • Coordinate budgets & resources; secure grants and partnerships to expand program offerings.
  • Design & deliver professional learning on PBIS, inclusive practices, and data-driven instruction.
  • Redesigned Corry Virtual Academy, cutting cyber placement costs by $800K+ annually.
  • Built two district-wide apps to track & approve teacher team-building hours.
  • Led integration of IXL & quarterly data reviews, achieving PSSA gains in ELA and Math.
  • Authored & managed district newsletter, strengthening communication and morale.
  • Developed & maintained Title I Schoolwide Plans, ensuring compliance and collaboration.
  • Taught CTE programming, networking, forensics, and honors science courses.
  • Secured a $500K tech grant to launch a summer CS program for minority students.
  • Facilitated after-school STEM clubs (robotics, coding, maker labs).
  • Analyzed academic & attendance data to inform RTI and PLC decisions.
  • Trained teachers in curriculum development and tech integration.
  • Collaborated on AI-driven voice analysis for early detection of neurological conditions.
  • Designed experiments & validated ML models, presenting findings at conferences.
